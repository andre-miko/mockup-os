## Scope

Sprout is a personal habit tracker. Users define habits with a daily
cadence and check them off each day. The v0.1 mockups cover a Today
view (today's pending and completed habits) and a Habits list (all
habits with their streak counts).

Out of scope for v0.1:

- Shared / social habits
- Detailed analytics or history graphs
- Reminders / notifications
- Mobile-native shell (responsive web only)
